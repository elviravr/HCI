VR Environments

Spatial Augmented Reality (SAR or Projection Mapping) uses everyday video projectors. You can project anything on any surface, to turn common 3D shaped objects into interactive displays. Spatial Augmented Reality can also be interactive.

Let's start with making a new earth

For the workshop we used the projection mapping software: MadMapper. We used a ball of polystyrene foam, which we projected on an image of the world. Music influenced the image, it turned more or less visible. Then we replaced the image with a rotating earth (a video) that made it look like it was actually turning.
